Data Notes
Data uploaded into the Registered Apprenticeship Partners Information Database System (RAPIDS) by the state. Apprentice counts are based on physical program location. State Totals are directly from FutureWorks PIRL data.

County Filter: Use the County dropdown to view apprenticeship data for specific counties within the selected state. Select "All Counties" to view statewide totals. County-level data integration requires detailed RAPIDS data by county location.

Terms & Definitions
Term Definition
Active Registered Apprentices in RAPIDS This reflects the total number of active Registered Apprentices for FY 2025 as reported in RAPIDS (defined above).
Co-Enrolled in a Registered Apprenticeship Program by WIOA Title I Funding Stream This reflects the co-enrollment rate for each of the WIOA Title I programs/funding streams (Adult, Dislocated Worker, and Youth) as determined by dividing, for example, the number of WIOA Adult participants reported as co-enrolled in Registered Apprenticeship (PIRL 931) by the total number of WIOA Title I Adult program participants served during the last four quarters ending 12/31/24.
Individual Training Account (ITA) ITA is marked if PIRL 1319, Established Individual Training Account (ITA), is 1=Yes.
On the Job Training (OJT) OJT is marked if the participant received OJT in any of the three training buckets (PIRL 1303, 1310, 1315 is marked as 01).
Participant An individual who has received a service funded by WIOA Title I other than self-service or information-only activities.
Participants Served Participants with a participation date prior to the end of the selected date range and whose exit date is blank or beyond the beginning of the selected date range. Any participant served by the program in that time period if only for a single day.
Percent of WIOA Title I Participants Served who are Co-Enrolled in Registered Apprenticeship This reflects the co-enrollment rate as determined by dividing the 931 co-enrollment number by the total number of WIOA Title I participants served during the last four quarters ending 12/31/24.
PIRL The Participant Individual Record Layout is a USDOL-mandated reporting layout that provides a standardized set of data elements, definitions, and reporting instructions to describe the characteristics, activities, and outcomes of individual WIOA Title I participants. It is the source of the WIOA Title I data in this Dashboard.
RAPIDS RAPIDS is the Registered Apprenticeship Partners Information Database System. RAPIDS serves as the primary platform for RA sponsors to manage their Registered Apprentices, occupations, job openings, and other relevant program information.
Received RA Training Received RA Training is equal to the total of PIRL 1303, 1310, and 1315, where 09 = Registered Apprenticeship.
Received Registered Apprenticeship Training This reflects the data recorded in PIRL elements 1303, 1310, and 1315. PIRL instructions for these elements state: PIRL #1303 Type of Training 1, PIRL #1310 Type of Training 2, PIRL #1315 Type of Training 3 must be value 9 in any of the three Types of Training elements.
Registered Apprenticeship (RA) RA is measured by PIRL 931 - Apprenticeship program = 1 if the participant entered into a Registered Apprenticeship program or if the participant was a registered apprentice at the time of program entry.
Support Services Support services is marked if there is a date in PIRL 1409 - showing a participant received supportive services, including but not limited to transportation, child care, dependent care, and housing that are necessary to engage the participant to participate in programs that provide career training.
Type of Training Service Type of Training Service - the first (PIRL 1303), the second (PIRL 1310), and third (PIRL 1315) training service the participant received.
WIOA Title I Adults An individual who is 18 years or older and is a participant in the WIOA Title I adult program as defined above.
WIOA Title I Dislocated Worker An individual who meets the eligibility criteria for a dislocated worker eligibility criteria for a Dislocated Worker and is a participant in the WIOA Title I Dislocated Worker program.
WIOA Title I Funding Streams This refers to the three WIOA Title I streams of funding for the Title I Adult, Dislocated Worker, and Youth programs.
WIOA Title I Participants For purposes of the dashboard, WIOA Title I is limited to the WIOA Title I programs that serve Adults, Dislocated Workers, and Youth. Adults, Dislocated Workers and Youth who receive services funded under WIOA Title I other than self-service or information-only activities are considered participants.
WIOA Title I Participants Co-Enrolled in Registered Apprenticeship This reflects the data recorded in PIRL element 931. PIRL instructions for this element state: Record 1 if the participant entered into a Registered Apprenticeship program or if the participant was a registered apprentice at the time of program entry. Record 0 if the participant did not enter into a Registered Apprenticeship program. Record 9 if grantee is unable to track enrollment in the program.
WIOA Title I Participants Served during the last four quarters ending 12/31/24 WIOA Title I Adult, Dislocated Worker and Youth participants served even if only for a single day prior to the end of 12/31/24 and whose exit date is blank or beyond the beginning of the selected date range.
WIOA Title I Youth An individual who meets the eligibility criteria for a Youth and is a participant in the WIOA Title I Youth program.
